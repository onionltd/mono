About

vworp! started of as a solution to a common problem: How to link to a web service which may live on many addresses but only a few are online at a time.

Many, if not all, high-traffic services utilize two or more onion addresses to stave off DDoS attacks. If unsuccessful and an address goes down, one of the others usually takes its place. For habitual users, it's become a common practice to visit the so-called link directories to find the online addresses. This may work for users but not so much for content publishers who want to link to a specific content.

This is where vworp! comes in. Use it to link to a content in your forum post or on your website and be sure the readers will always end up where you want them to be. Start by pasting a URL to vworp!.

The list of supported services and its addresses is limited by content of OnionTree – an open source repository of Tor hidden services. In other words, you can't link to just any service.
