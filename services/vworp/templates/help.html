Help

1. My link is not recognized, why?

The list of supported services is limited by content of OnionTree – an open source repository of Tor hidden services. Your best shot at making vworp! support a new service is to have it included in OnionTree.

2. What's a preview link?

A preview link is a link that requires user's interaction before it redirects. You can recognize them by ?preview at the end.

3. All mirrors are offline.

vworp! is unable to redirect you to the service because all of the service's mirrors are down. Try again later.
