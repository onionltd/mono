Privacy policy

Version: 1.0 | Last modified: 2020-04-19

This is the very first draft of the policy. If you feel like something is not properly addressed, let me know.

Logs

The server is configured to log incoming HTTP requests. The information included in the logs is:

  • Date and Time of the request
  • Visited URL
  • User agent

The information from the logs is used to assess the number of visitors. I may or may not keep the logs indefinitely.

URL hits

vworp! keeps count of visits of a shortened URL. This information is used internally to build various statistics.
